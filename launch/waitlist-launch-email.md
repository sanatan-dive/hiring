# Waitlist Launch Email

Send 2 hours before your X launch tweet. Subscribers seeing the launch
ahead of the public is part of why they signed up.

## Subject lines (A/B test if you have > 200)

- A: `Hirin is live (you're early)`
- B: `Your job matcher is ready`
- C: `Day 0 — Hirin opens to early users`

## From

`Sanatan from Hirin <hello@hirin.app>`

## Body

```
Hey [first name],

Thanks for being on the waitlist — Hirin' is live as of today.

If you've been waiting to upload your resume and stop scrolling job boards,
this is the moment.

[Big button: Sign up and upload resume → https://hirin.app/sign-up]

What you get on the free tier:
  • Weekly digest of the top job matches for your resume
  • Pulls from RemoteOK and WeWorkRemotely
  • 1 resume, 5 active applications tracked
  • 7-day match history

Want daily + AI cover letter + interview prep + unlimited tracking + access
to all 6 sources? Pro is $9/mo (or ₹699 in India). Cancel anytime, 14-day
refund if you don't use Pro features.

[Smaller button: See pricing → https://hirin.app/pricing]

I'm a one-person team and I'd love your feedback. Reply to this email
with anything that's broken, confusing, or missing. I read every reply.

Free Pro for 3 months for the first 50 waitlist members who reply with
the role they're hunting. (Just hit reply.)

Thanks for trusting me with this,
Sanatan

—
You're getting this because you signed up at hirin.app's waitlist.
[Unsubscribe] · [Manage preferences]
```

## What the email is doing

- Single primary CTA (sign up)
- Secondary CTA (pricing) for the curious
- Personal close with reply prompt — emails that get replies become future
  conversations, not just one-off blasts
- Free Pro offer to the first 50 — drives immediate engagement
- Unsubscribe link explicit — required by CAN-SPAM/GDPR + you wired this
  in `/unsubscribe`

## After sending

- Watch reply rate. > 5% reply is excellent for a launch email.
- Personally reply to every "this looks great" with a direct ask: "what
  would make this most useful for you?"
- Track CTR via UTM: `?utm_source=launch_email&utm_campaign=waitlist`
